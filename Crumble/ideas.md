# Design Brainstorm: Electric Mimos Martian Stone Fruit Crumble

## Context
This is an HIA (Human Development Mathematics Insight Academy) Exercise Æssay — a recipe that is simultaneously a pedagogical instrument, a phase-state demonstration, and a culinary record. The visual design must honor all three dimensions.

## Approach A — Thermal Cartography
**Design Movement:** Scientific field notebook meets alchemical manuscript
**Core Principles:** Every element is a measurement; warmth is structural not decorative; the page breathes like a living document
**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#0e0c0a), copper oxide (#c8703a), aged parchment (#e8dcc8), ember glow (#9a5228)
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column — a narrow left rail of timestamps/labels, a wide right field of content. Like a lab notebook.
**Signature Elements:** Thin copper rule lines as structural dividers; monospaced timestamps in the left rail; Playfair Display for headings, Space Grotesk for body
**Interaction Philosophy:** Serving adjuster is a precise instrument, not a playful slider. Numbers update with a brief opacity fade.
**Animation:** Minimal — only the quantity values animate on change (opacity 0.4 → 1, 200ms)
**Typography:** Playfair Display (headings, field review quote), Space Grotesk 300 (body), monospace for timestamps

## Approach B — Martian Terroir
**Design Movement:** Brutalist editorial with warm geological palette
**Core Principles:** The page is a specimen; ingredients are geological strata; time is measured not felt
**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0a0806), rust (#b85c2a), bone (#d4c4a8), iron (#6b4c3b)
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed sections separated by thick rule lines; ingredient groups as geological cross-sections
**Signature Elements:** Large section numbers in the left margin; ingredient quantities in a distinct copper monospace; a single decorative dodecahedron watermark at the footer
**Interaction Philosophy:** The serving stepper is a dial — minimal, precise, satisfying
**Animation:** Quantity values slide up on change (translateY -4px → 0, 250ms ease-out)
**Typography:** Space Grotesk 500 (headings), Space Grotesk 300 (body), Space Mono (quantities/timestamps)

## Approach C — Alchemical Protocol
**Design Movement:** Laboratory logbook meets sacred geometry
**Core Principles:** The recipe is a ritual; each phase is a transformation; the page holds the memory of the event
**Color Philosophy:** Obsidian (#0c0a08), burnished copper (#c87840), cream (#ead8bc), deep amber (#7a4820)
**Layout Paradigm:** Single centered column, wide margins, generous vertical rhythm. The page feels like a document meant to be read slowly.
**Signature Elements:** Eyebrow labels in small-caps copper; protocol steps with left-aligned time codes; a field review blockquote with a copper left border
**Interaction Philosophy:** The serving control is a quiet instrument at the top — understated, functional, precise
**Animation:** Quantities fade through opacity on change; no movement
**Typography:** Playfair Display italic (field review, section intros), Space Grotesk 300 (body), Space Grotesk 500 (labels/eyebrows)

## Selected Approach: A — Thermal Cartography

The asymmetric two-column lab notebook layout is the most faithful to the HIA portal aesthetic seen in the homepage screenshot — dark background, copper accents, small-caps eyebrow labels, Playfair Display headings. It also honors the recipe's nature as a timed protocol: the left timestamp rail makes the 83-minute structure immediately legible as a visual architecture, not just a list of steps.
