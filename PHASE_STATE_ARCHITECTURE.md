### LENS 02 :: CATEGORY THEORY AND EMERGING POSSIBILITY

Category Theory is the study of **relationships between structures**, not just the structures themselves. The *morphisms* (transformations) are more important than the objects. The structure is revealed not in what something *is*, but in how it can *become* something else.

**Bifurcation as Morphism Branching:**
A bifurcation point is where the morphism structure radically reorganizes. Before, there is one path. At the bifurcation, the morphism splits. Suddenly, two futures become mathematically real. In human terms: The breakthrough moment. 

**The Feedback Loop:**
Observation creates Morphism. When we interact with these attractors, we are not merely observing a fixed space. We are **co-creating it**. Observation reveals morphisms that existed mathematically but were invisible until actualized.
