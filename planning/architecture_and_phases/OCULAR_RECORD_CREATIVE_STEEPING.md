# OCULAR RECORD :: CREATIVE STEEPING WEBSITE
## A Vision Architecture Extraction of the Cousin Project

> *This document serves as the visual and conceptual ledger of the Creative Steeping portal. It ensures aesthetic coherence and tracks the shared DNA between the Phase-State Observatory and the Steeping rituals.*

### 1. THE TEXTUAL PHILOSOPHY (ALCHEMICAL DNA)

*   **The Equation of Non-Doing:** `PATIENCE x PROCRASTINATION = STEEPING`. A profound reframing of avoidance into active, observational preparation.
*   **The Covenant of Resonance:** *"Your trace stays. Not your name. Not your data. Your resonance."* This validates the shift from an ego-driven ecosystem to a collective, vibrational registry.
*   **The Gateway Inquiries:** 
    *   *"Who do I Think I Am? Your answer opens the steep."* 
    *   *"What is ready to be transmuted within me?"*
    *   *"Traveler."*
*   **The Functional Ledger:** `[ OFFER AN INQUIRY ]`, `[ THE LEDGER ]`, `[ ACTIVE PAUSE ]`. Time intervals are strictly modular (`5M`, `15M`, `22M`).

### 2. THE TYPOGRAPHIC DIALECTIC (ROCK & OCEAN)

The typography is built entirely on the conceptual metaphor of The Union of Rock and Ocean:
*   **The Ocean (The Soul Axis):** High-contrast, elegant **Serif Italics** (e.g., *Playfair Display*). Used for visceral, resonant questions and conversational pacing. It flows.
*   **The Rock (The Code Axis):** Thin, widely-tracked, all-caps **Monospace / Sans-Serif**. This is the architectural infrastructure. Used for buttons, functional bounds `[ ]`, and structural readouts. It holds the line.

### 3. THE PALETTE OF THE VOID

*   **BASE (The Canvas):** Absolute Black (`#0B0B0B` to `#000000`). Deep space, eliminating edges and creating an infinite, lensless AR depth.
*   **TERRA (The Spark):** Golden-Ochre / Terra Cotta (`#C4622D`). Used for active states, brackets, sacred wireframes, and focal text (The Rock).
*   **LUNA (The Light):** Moonlight Off-White / Cream (`#FDFBE2`). Ensures high-contrast readability for the narrative flow without the harsh digital burn of pure white (The Ocean).

### 4. GEOMETRIC ARCHITECTURE & CONTAINMENT

*   **The Field:** A subtle background grid of small gold dots acts as a coordinate map, subtly referencing the invisible intersections of the Dodecahedral Bloom.
*   **The Negative Void:** Massive, dramatic scale shifts and extreme "breathing room" (margins). Content is often centered like a solitary beacon in deep space.
*   **The Sacred Wireframes:** Interactive boxes and main content frames are held within thin golden Hexagonal/Dodecahedral borders. The HUD (Heads-Up Display) feels lightweight, ancient, and highly technical simultaneously.
