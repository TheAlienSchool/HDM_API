**Why Now:**

**Primary Hypothesis:** Humanity's evolution is not linear but occurs through distinct, geometrically-determined phases of collective maturation. **Executive Summary**

**The Core Hypothesis**

Humanity's evolution is not linear but occurs through recurring, qualitatively distinct phases of collective consciousness maturation. These phases can be understood through a 12-archetypal framework inspired by Jungian psychology, Gebser's structures of consciousness, and indigenous wisdom traditions (e.g., Haudenosaunee Seven Generations principle). Each phase represents a full cycle of preparation, emergence, integration, and generativity across 30-50 year blocks (one "generation").
