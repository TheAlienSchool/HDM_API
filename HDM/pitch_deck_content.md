**Headline:** Four Signals That Predict Organizational Health

**Body:** PING's Pattern Analysis Engine quantifies four core resonance metrics, each derived from the principles of HDM and validated through organizational behavior research. **Response Velocity** measures the speed and consistency of replies — not just average response time, but the *pattern* of it, revealing urgency, trust, and priority. **Initiation Ratio** maps who starts conversations versus who only reacts, revealing leadership dynamics and potential bottlenecks. **Reciprocity Score** measures the balance of exchange — is this a true dialogue or a one-way broadcast? **Silence Detection** is perhaps the most powerful metric: it identifies individuals and teams whose communication has gone quiet, a leading indicator of disengagement, burnout, or unresolved conflict — weeks before it surfaces in a survey.
