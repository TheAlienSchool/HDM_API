**Added:** Clear attribution in the opening epigraph:

Just as Bhante G brought clarity to the practice of observing the mind, this book aims to bring clarity to the practice of participating in the relational field. ## Summary of Impact

### Legal Safety
- All high-risk chapter titles changed
- Structural sequence sufficiently differentiated
- Clear attribution to source of inspiration
- **Result:** Copyright-safe, legally defensible

### Educational Depth
- Scientific grounding added early
- Cross-cultural validation throughout
- Diverse voices included
- Bibliography provides pathways for deeper learning
- **Result:** More credible, more accessible to diverse audiences

### Pedagogical Strength
- Maintains clarity and accessibility of original
- Adds depth without adding complexity
- Creates multiple entry points for different backgrounds
- Positions book as gateway to larger conversation
- **Result:** Stronger teaching tool, wider appeal

---

## What Remains Unchanged

- The core teaching of the Seven Octaves
- The warm, accessible tone
- The practical, experiential approach
- The first-person voice for practices
- The emphasis on feeling over thinking
- The structure of moving from Why → What → How → Integration

**The essence of the book is preserved; only the scaffolding and depth have been enhanced.**
