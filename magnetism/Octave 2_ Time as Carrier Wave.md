### The Octave in Exploration

Today, I explore the second **Octave** of Relational Living: **Time as Carrier Wave**. I am releasing the illusion of time as a linear track of moments that I chase, lose, or run out of. Instead, I am experiencing time as the very medium of existence, the substance that allows for all becoming. It is the vast, silent ocean upon which the **Transmission** of life travels. It is not passing me by; it is carrying me.

> **A Living Glossary:** **Time as Carrier Wave** is the understanding of time not as a measure of duration, but as the fundamental medium that carries the patterns of existence. Like the water that allows a wave to travel, time is what allows the music of reality to be heard, felt, and experienced.

He is the guardian of my rhythm, the keeper of my internal clock, which is attuned not to minutes and hours, but to the great cycles of life.
