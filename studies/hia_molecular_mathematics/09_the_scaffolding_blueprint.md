To integrate the FIA, we will utilize `app.js` to create a "Gated Insight" architecture powered by PJAX (asynchronous page loading).

**Phase 1: The Somatic Grounding (HIA Gateway)**
When a user arrives at the Academy, they do not immediately see the 3D fractal matrix. Instead, `app.js` loads a deeply grounding, 2D typographic environment. Here, they are introduced to the *Humanity in Action* philosophy. They learn the basic vocabulary—what it means to "Active Pause" and what "Synarchy" implies. 

**Phase 2: The Continuous Sonnet**
Because `app.js` manages state across the entire site without hard-reloading the browser (PJAX), the **Sonnet Audio Engine (528Hz)** initiates on the very first page. As the user reads the foundational philosophy, their nervous system begins to synchronize to the Triplicate Resonance.

**Phase 3: The Membrane Crossing**
Once the user completes the introductory grounding module, `app.js` unlocks the gateway to the FIA. Because the audio context and somatic state are already established, clicking the link to the *Fractal Insights Explorer* does not feel like loading a new webpage. It feels like stepping through a physical door.
The 2D text fades, the 3D WebGL canvas initializes natively within the container, and the camera plunges the user directly into the center of the 41st Emanation node.

This scaffolding ensures that the FIA remains a sacred, advanced tool—accessible only once the user's nervous system and vocabulary have been properly tuned by the *Humanity in Action* protocol.
